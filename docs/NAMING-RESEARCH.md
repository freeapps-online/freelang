# App Naming Research

## App Description

Voice-first AI language learning app with 4 modes:
1. **Practice** - speak in your language, hear AI translation, repeat, get rated on pronunciation
2. **Flashcards** - swipe to match translations, score tracking
3. **Live Translation** - dual-mic real-time translation for conversations with native speakers
4. **Conversation** - free chat with AI in target language

## Requirements
- Short, playful, says "language"
- Cheap TLD domain (.online, .site, .app)
- Not taken by any existing language/translation app

## Key Insight

The language app naming space is one of the most saturated in tech. Every obvious combination of Lang/Lingo/Lingua/Speak/Talk + anything is taken. After 70+ checks, **Languize** is the only name with all 8 domains free and zero meaningful conflicts. The unchecked names above (Flingo, Parli, Slango, etc.) represent unexplored directions worth investigating.
